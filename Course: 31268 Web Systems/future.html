My Future

Aspirations

My ultimate career goal is to land a fulfilling role in the evolving field of cybersecurity. The nature of cyber threats and the constant challenge of staying one step ahead of hackers is what truly captivates me. I'm particularly fascinated by the field of ethical hacking and penetration testing, where I can use my skills to identify vulnerabilities and help organisations strengthen their defenses.

Beyond my cybersecurity aspirations, I'm also passionate about exploring different avenues for generating income. I believe in the importance of diversifying income streams and having multiple sources of financial stability. Currently, I'm experimenting with various side hustles, from online tutoring to investing in the stock market. It's exciting to learn new skills and discover unconventional ways to earn money while also building a safety net for the future.

One of my biggest aspirations in life is to have the freedom to travel anywhere, anytime. The world is full of incredible places to explore, and I have a long list of destinations I dream of visiting. Two places that particularly capture my imagination are Switzerland and Italy. The breathtaking landscapes of the Swiss Alps, with their snow-capped peaks and charming villages, seem like something out of a fairytale. And the rich history, vibrant culture, and delicious cuisine of Italy have always held a special allure for me.
